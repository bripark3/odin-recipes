Spicy Garlic Potato Noodles

Description

This is a recipe for spicy garlic potato noodles. Good for people who may have gluten allergies and are unable to eat regular noodles.

Ingredients

Noodles:

  • 1 cup potato starch
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup boiling water

Garlic Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons ketchup
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup potato starch, salt,and vegetable oil in a bowl. Add a splash of boiling water at a time, stirring between additions, until you get a perfectly textured dough.
  2. Transfer to a work surface dusted with a little potato starch. Working quickly while the dough is still warm, knead, adding more potato starch if necessary, until smooth and elastic.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Set out a large bowl of cold water.
  4. While the water is boiling roll dogh to a thickness of 1/8 inch. Cut into 1/4 inch noodles with a pizza cutter. Seperate noodles
  5. Add potato noodles and cook until they float, about 1 minute. Transfer to the bowl of cold water. Repeat to cook remaining noodles.
  6. whisk oyster sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, ketchup, sesame oil, sugar, and sriracha together in a bowl.
  7. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add Garlic and sizzle for 15 seconds. Add 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes and green onions; stir-fry for just a few seconds. Before the garlic starts to brown add in the potato noodles. Its okay that some water comes into the pan as it will stop the cooking process and prevent the garlic from burning
  8. Add sauce and stir until noodles are coated. Cook until noodles are heated through and sauce starts to simmer, 1 to 2 minutes. Turn off the heat and stur in the cilantro.
  9. Divide noodles into 2 bowls and garnish with more cilantro and red pepper flakes.
