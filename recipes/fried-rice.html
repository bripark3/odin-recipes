Chicken and Bacon Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for chicken and bacon fried rice, which is one of my favorite dishes of all time.

Ingredients
  • 3 cups water
  • 3 cups instant white rice
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts cut into chunks
  • 12 slices bacon
  • 1 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce, or to taste
Steps
  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Stir instant white rice into boiling water. Cook rice until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a seperate skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken in hot oil until cooked through and no longer pink in the middle, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Fry bacon in a large wok or skillet over medium-high until hot and cooked through, but not yet crunchy. 2 to 3 minutes per side. Remove bacon to a plate lined with a paper towel. Drain fat from wok reserving 1 to 2 tablespoons of drippings in the wok. Cut bacon into small pieces.
  4. Return wok to medium-high heat and heat bacon drippings. Combine rice,chicken,bacon,green onions, and soy sauce in wok; cook and stir until evenly mixed and hot, 2 to 3 minutes.
